Brown's first official student group focusing on the intersection of computer science and social impact.

About CS+Social Good

Our mission is to empower students at Brown to leverage technology for social good by inspiring action, facilitating collaboration and forging pathways toward change. We envision a world where technology helps us solve some of our biggest problems. And to make that vision a reality, we're promoting a culture shift in which a new generation of tinkerers, thinkers and doers can build meaningful ideas and make a change.

We're at unique point in history where computer science is one of the fastest growing fields â€” not only at Brown, but in the world. Inspired by CS+Social Good at Stanford, we wanted to take initiative to put some of the talent, potential, and energy from our community on College Hill and invest it into bettering the world. What started with one undergrad on an opposite coast is growing into a multi-university effort to help students make the world a better place by providing the support, resources and guidance to do so.
